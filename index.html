Who I Am

Hello! I'm Igor Szuniewicz, a multi-faceted creative professional operating at the intersection of music composition, audio engineering, and software development. Based in West Flanders, Belgium, I specialize in crafting immersive and interactive audio experiences, particularly for games and new media.

My passion lies in leveraging technology to push creative boundaries. Whether it's designing intricate interactive music systems, implementing realistic 3D spatial audio, or developing custom audio plugins (VSTs), I strive to create sound that not only enhances the narrative but becomes an integral part of the user experience. I have a strong foundation in both the artistic and technical aspects of audio, comfortable working within game engines like Unreal and Unity, utilizing middleware like Wwise and FMOD, and programming with languages such as C++, Python, and C#.
